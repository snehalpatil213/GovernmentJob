Indian Railways conducts recruitment through the Railway Recruitment Boards (RRB) and Railway Recruitment Cells (RRC) for various technical and non-technical posts across India. Railway jobs offer excellent career growth, job security, attractive salary and additional government benefits.

Key Points :

  • Recruitment through RRB and RRC across different railway zones.
  • Vacancies available for NTPC, Group D, ALP, Technician and JE posts.
  • Transparent selection through CBT, PET and Document Verification.
  • Attractive salary, pension benefits, railway passes and career growth.
